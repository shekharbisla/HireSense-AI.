How to Use HireSense AI

Follow these steps to get the best out of HireSense AI:

  1. Paste Resume: Copy your resume text and paste it into the input box on the home page.
  2. Parse Resume (AI): Click the “Parse Resume” button to extract your Name, Email, Phone, Skills, and Education.
  3. Find Matching Jobs: Click “Find Matching Jobs” to see job roles that align with your skills.
  4. Review Results: Check the extracted skills vs job requirements. Green chips show where you are a match.
  5. Apply: Use the job links or suggestions to apply directly.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use a clean text version of your resume (avoid too many tables or images).
  • Keep skills section updated (Python, SQL, ML, etc.).
  • Use “Parse Resume” first to confirm extraction looks correct.

Need more help? Check the Terms page or contact the developer.
